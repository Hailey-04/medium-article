3 Easy ways to make honey face masks for every skin type

Beat the Covid-19 Lockdown blues with a healthy and organic skin care routine

Why do you need this?

Are trying to continue taking good care of your skin especially during these "trying times" and just don't want to spend any more money? You've come to the right place! Bellow are the 3 easiest DIY face masks you can make with honey and one or two more foods you'll find in your kitchen.

1. Clears Spots and Scars

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp raw honey
  • juice from half a lemon

Directions

  • Mix the honey and the lemon in a bowl.
  • Apply the mask to your face and neck while avoiding your eyes and the area around it.
  • Leave the mask on for 20 minutes.
  • Wash off with warm water, followed by cold water to close your pores.
  • Pat Dry.

Tips!

  • Before you do this mask make sure your skin is not sensitive to lemon.
  • Test it on another part of your body, like your elbow, first.

2. Remove Blackheads

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp Raw Honey

Directions

  • Mix the ingredients in a bowl.
  • Apply the mask to your face.
  • Leave it on for 10-15 minutes.
  • Rinse it off with lukewarm water.

Tips!

  • This mask should not be repeated more than 2x a week as cinnamon may irritate the skin.
  • Rinse it off immediately if your skin gets irritated and don't use this face mask.

3. Glowing Skin

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Aloe Vera
  • 2 tbsp Raw Honey

Directions

  • Mix the ingredients in a bowl until it is smooth.
  • Wash and exfoliate your face using a gentle scrub.
  • Apply it to your skin and leave it on for 20-30 minutes.
  • Wash the mask off and apply your face mask to lock in the moisture.

Tips!

  • It is best to use freshly extracted aloe gel.
  • If you buy gel online, try your best to make sure it is pure with no artificial scents or colors.
